It includes two smgs and two shotguns between 900 -1600 credits. The to the right of that is the rifles. Anything from a burst rifle to an AK. These guns are the most popular and used the most by pros. Next are the snipers and heavy weapons. Snipers are very self explanatory and the heavy weapons are machine guns with maximum recoil.
